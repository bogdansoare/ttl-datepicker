DateTime Picker

Your task is to critique and suggest improvements to the date time picker below, and have a go at prototyping your suggestions.

Our expectation is that you spend at least an hour on the task.

The criteria is as followed:

  • No restriction in terms of approach, technologies or design.
  • It must at least fulfil the following criteria:
    • Default should be to today at 09:00.
    • Be able to set date to today or tomorrow.
    • The output should present your selection in the format given.

Collect your thoughts...

I improved the UX by reducing the number of inputs upfront, using progressive disclosure for inputs like today/tomorrow.

For the UI I borrowed elements from the Googles material design, improving the experience with animations and a better design & structure.

Regarding technology stack, I used web components because I believe they are the future along with the Polymer library. I like working with web components because they give me flexibility, encapsulation; I can quickly use a component from one project into another.
